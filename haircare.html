Haircare Routine 💇‍♀️✨

From root to tip — simple steps for healthy, shiny hair

💖 The Basics

Healthy hair starts with consistency, not expensive products.

Focus on hydration, gentle washing, and protection from heat.

🧴 Wash Routine

• Shampoo only on scalp

• Conditioner only on ends

• Don’t wash every day

✨ Common Mistakes

❌ Too much heat without protection
❌ Oil on roots
❌ Overwashing hair

🌸 Glow Tips

✨ Use the two-coin method
✨ Use a silk pillowcase or a bonett to reduce frizz
✨ Hair mask twice a week
